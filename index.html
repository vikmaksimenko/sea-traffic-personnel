Welcome to Sea Traffic Personnel

we are professional crew management company

Sea Traffic Personnel LTD was founded in 2016 and located in Nikolaev, Ukraine.

Sea Traffic Personnel LTD manages a range of vessels including bulk carriers, container vessels, general cargo carriers, oil tankers, gas carriers, and chemical tankers, ranging from 300 to 319,000 DWT - with many being young and energy-efficient with an age profile below the industry average.

With unique expertise in maritime personnel, we really know how to manage crewing requests successfully. Our professional seafarers are at your service.

Our database contains more than 40000 highly qualified seafarers from Ukraine, Georgia and Russia for different vessel types including:

  • Tankers (of different types),
  • LPG,
  • Bulk Carriers,
  • Container vessels,
  • Dry Cargo,
  • Passenger Ships

We are always glad to welcome new qualified seamen who are interested in long-term cooperation.

We will be very glad to serve you on any matters, please do not hesitate to contact us.
